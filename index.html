All about turtles!

Did you know? (source: Wikipedia)

  • Turtles are reptiles of the order Testudines characterized by a special bony or cartilaginous shell developed from their ribs and acting as a shield.
  • Turtles are cold-blooded.
  • Turtles are divided into two groups, according to how they retract their necks into their shells.

Snnnnnakes!

Did you know? (source: Wikipedia)

  • Snakes are elongated, legless, carnivorous reptiles.
  • Living snakes are found on every continent except Antarctica.
  • Most species are nonvenomous.

Insects (gross)

Did you know? (source: Wikipedia)

  • Insects are hexapod invertebrates.
  • Nearly all insects hatch from eggs.
  • Adult insects typically move about by walking, flying, or sometimes swimming.
